ASSASSINATION CLASSROOM
Assassination Classroom follows the daily life of an extremely powerful octopus-like being working as a junior high homeroom teacher, and his students are dedicated to the task of assassinating him to prevent Earth from being destroyed. The students are considered "misfits" in their school and are taught in a separate building; the class he teaches is called 3-E. These same students greet him every day with assassination attempts in their journey to save the world by eventually murdering their own teacher, and earning 10 billion yen from the government.
CHARACTERS
Korosensei - He is an anti-hero and the homeroom teacher of Class 3-E. Has the physical features of a yellow octopus and he is also very powerful with a speed of Mach-20.
Nagisa - He is the main protagonist. He is also the best assassin in class 3-E. At the start of the series he has a lack of goals and low self esteem, but he slowly gained more self esteem and found his goals.
Karma - He is a protagonist and one of Nagisa's closest friends. Despite being known for his violent behavior, mischievious personality and troublesome attitude, he has good values and can be hardworking.
